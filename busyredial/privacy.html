Permissions required and why:

Busy Redial requires some authorizations to work. Here is described what they are and why:

  • RECEIVE_BOOT_COMPLETED: required for enable redial services
  • READ_PHONE_STATE: required to analize call duration. This permits to detect a busy call
  • CALL_PHONE: required for redial
  • VIBRATE: required for activate the vibrate mode
  • PROCESS_OUTGOING_CALL: required for redial
  • READ_CONTACTS: required for provide contact information
  • READ_EXTERNAL_STORAGE: required for play ringtone or notification tone in case it is stored on external storage
  • BILLING: required for check unlocker purchase (old unlocker version was provided via in-app purchase
  • ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE: required for check data connection state
