Permissions required and why:

Call Filter Alarm requires some authorizations to work. Here is described what they are and why.
We do not knowingly collect or transfer outside your phone any personal data accessed through these permissions. Permissions requested below are only used to provide functionality within Call Filter Alarm.

  • READ_PHONE_STATE: required for the call filter
  • READ_CONTACTS: required for contacts selection
  • READ_EXTERNAL_STORAGE: required for play ringtone or notification tone in case it is stored on external storage
  • RECEIVE_BOOT_COMPLETED: required for restore alarms on phone boot
  • CHANGE_WIFI_STATE: required for wifi managing
  • ACCESS_WIFI_STATE: required for check wifi state
  • VIBRATE: required for vibration
  • BLUETOOTH and BLUETOOTH_ADMIN: required for bluetooth managing
  • ACCESS_NOTIFICATION_POLICY: required to exit the Don't disturb profile (needed to restore ringer mode)
